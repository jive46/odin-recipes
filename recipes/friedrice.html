Fried Rice

Description

Singapore Fried Rice is a Chinese takeaway classic. It is a delicious amalgamation of fresh ginger, garlic, spicy roasted red chilli paste, and a tinge of Russian sauce (mayo and ketchup) for a burst of flavours! It is what a lot of people would consider the ultimate Chinese takeaway comfort food. A true hearty one-plate-meal by itself!

Ingredients

  • 200g Long Grain Rice (Boiled)
  • 1/4 tsp Chili Powder
  • 2 tbsp Red Chili Paste
  • 1/4 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1/4 tsp Curry Powder
  • 1 tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 100g Prawns
  • 2pcs Spring Onions (Roughly Chopped)
  • 1 clove Garlic (Sliced)
  • 3 tbsp Sunflower Oil
  • 1pc Large Chili (Sliced)
  • 1 inch Organic Ginger (Grated)
  • 1 tbsp Sesame Oil
  • 1pc Clove
  • White Pepper (A Pinch)
  • 1pc Egg
  • 1 handful Carrots or Peas (Optional)

Steps

  1. Heat 1 tbsp. of sunflower oil in the pan, flash fry the prawns, garlic, ginger, chilli paste, chillies, spring onions, and set aside.

  2. Whisk the egg, then fry it in a little oil. Roll and slice the omelette into 1cm strips.

  3. Heat the remaining sunflower oil in a wok for 5 minutes. Add the set-aside ingredients immediately, followed by the rice and vegetables (carrots or peas).

  4. Grind the clove and add to the mix with chilli powder, white pepper, turmeric, and curry powder.

  5. Ensure everything is well integrated, including the omelette, before seasoning the rice with soy sauce and sesame oil.
